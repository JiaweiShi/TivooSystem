Music Lecture: "How Josquin Sounded: An Exercise in Musical Anthropology"

Start time: 10/01/11 11:00
End time: 10/01/11 12:00
Description: Michael Long (University of Buffalo) received the B.A. degree, summa cum laude, from Amherst College and the M.F.A. and Ph.D. degrees from Princeton University. His essays on late medieval music have appeared in the Journal of the American Musicological Society; Early Music History; and others. His book, "Beautiful Monsters: Imagining the Classic in Musical Media," was published in 2008 by the University of California Press.
