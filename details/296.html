DukeGEN: Live from Silicon Valley

Start time: 09/30/11 04:00
End time: 09/30/11 05:30
Description: Ryan Spoon graduated from Duke in 2003 and co-founded beRecruited. He later sold beRecruited and has since worked for Polaris Ventures, one of the top venture capital firms in the world. He runs Dogpatch Labs, an incubator in San Francisco. Ryan will share lessons learned and answer questions from those looking to start their own company.
