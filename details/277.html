Doxita 4: Short Documentaries on Tour

Start time: 09/30/11 07:00
End time: 09/30/11 09:00
Description: This is the fourth season for Doxita, a traveling festival of documentary films that are under 40 minutes in length.?The program represents a wide variety of documentary (domestic and foreign, super-short and longer format, serious and funny), and is designed to profile the great content and artistic vision that nonfiction short films provide, but that people don?t often get a chance to see.?
